Reference design, Magnetic Stripe Reader

Jun, 2012 to August, 2013
4 minutes read

A magnetic stripe card is a type of card capable of storing data by modifying the magnetism of tiny iron-based magnetic particles on a band of magnetic material on the card. The data on the stripe is read by swiping against a magnetic reading head. Magnetic stripe cards are commonly used in credit cards, identity cards, and transportation tickets.
